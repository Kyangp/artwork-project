One Million Marks
About How it works

A million people. One artwork.

Each person places one permanent mark.

What is this?

This is the internet’s largest collaborative artwork. A single canvas created by people from all over the world, where each participant makes one tiny artistic decision and leaves one permanent mark.

What happens when a million strangers each make one tiny artistic decision? No one knows — and that uncertainty is the point. Once a mark is placed, it cannot be changed, removed, or corrected. There are no edits, no revisions, and no second chances.

When the final mark is placed, the artwork is complete forever. It will never change again. This artwork exists only because people choose to participate — each mark equal, each decision final, each contribution part of something larger than itself.
