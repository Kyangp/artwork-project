Before you place your mark

This is a one-time contribution. Please read carefully.

  • You will place one mark on the canvas.
  • Your mark is permanent — it cannot be edited or removed.
  • There are no retries, revisions, or second attempts.
  • For now, participation is limited to one mark per person.

When you confirm, you are choosing to make a small decision that will remain part of the artwork forever.
